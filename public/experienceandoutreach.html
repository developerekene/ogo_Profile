Master’s Student (2019-2021)
I completed the Pharmacognosy Master’s program in Faculty of Pharmacy at the University of Lagos College of Medicine. This program included coursework and laboratory work. My independent laboratory work was on “Evaluation of Sarcocephalus latifolius (sm.) E. A. and Sorghum bicolor linn. used in reduction of creatinine level and their in-vitro antioxidant activity“.
Courses included: Medical statistics, Advanced techniques in pharmacognosy, Cultivation, conservational biodiversity of medicinal plants and field training, Taxonomy and chemotaxonomy, Phototherapy and nutritional medicine, Agricultural and veterinary pharmacognosy, Clinical Pharmacognosy and training, Advanced phytochemistry, Pharmacognosic analysis and surgical dressing, Pharmaceutical analysis of natural drug product, Biological variation in natural products, Research project, and Research seminar.
